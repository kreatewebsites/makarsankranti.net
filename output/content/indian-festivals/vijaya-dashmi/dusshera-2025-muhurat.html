Significance of Vijay Muhurat

The Vijay Muhurat is regarded as the most favorable moment on Dussehra, believed to bring success to any undertaking begun during this time. Historically, kings would launch military campaigns during this period. In modern times, people conduct Shami Puja, initiate ventures, or invest in vehicles and properties during this auspicious interval.
