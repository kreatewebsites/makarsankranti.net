The Countdown: Timeline to Performance

3 Months Out: Foundation

Complete script and score. Conduct auditions and assign roles. Secure venue and essential crew.

2 Months Out: Creation

Start rehearsals. Begin set design and construction. Gather fabrics and craft costumes.

1 Month Out: Promotion

Run ad campaigns. Design posters and flyers. Boost social media activity and connect with local press.

2 Weeks Out: Integration

Start stage rehearsals, blending lighting, sound, sets, and costumes with the show.

Show Week: Final Polish

Run complete dress rehearsals. Finalize adjustments. Ready front-of-house and volunteers.
