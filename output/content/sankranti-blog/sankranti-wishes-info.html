Celebrated under various names in India, this festival signifies the sun's shift into Capricorn. It’s a joyful occasion of harvest gratitude, rich traditions, and vibrant unity across the nation.

A Symphony of Greetings

The spirit of the festival shines through India's myriad languages, each greeting distinct in tone and form, yet united in wishing joy and success for all.

Hindi

मकर संक्रांति की शुभकामनाएँ!

"Makar Sankranti ki shubhkamnayein!"

Tamil

பொங்கல் வாழ்த்துக்கள்!

"Pongal Vazhthukkal!"

Gujarati

ઉત્તરાયણની શુભકામનાઓ!

"Uttarayan ni shubhkamnao!"

Telugu

సంక్రాంతి శుభాకాంక్షలు!

"Sankranti Subhakankshalu!"

Punjabi

ਲੋਹੜੀ ਦੀਆਂ ਲੱਖ ਲੱਖ ਵਧਾਈਆਂ!

"Lohri diyan lakh lakh vadhaiyan!"

Marathi

तिळगुळ घ्या, गोड गोड बोला!

"Tilgul ghya, god god bola!"
